How to prepare
1

Think about your current follow-up process

How do leads find you? What happens after they opt in or reach out? Where do things fall through? You don't need a formal answer — just thinking about it before the call means we can move faster.

2

Have your current tool stack in mind

What CRM, email platform, or funnel tool do you currently use (if any)? We'll be building in GoHighLevel, but knowing where you're starting helps us plan the migration or starting point.

3

Write down your top 3 operational bottlenecks

What are the 3 things you know you should be doing in your marketing or sales process but aren't? These are exactly what the AI COO System is built to handle — we'll map it to your specific gaps on the call.

What to expect on the call: 30 minutes. We'll show you the live AI COO System running in real time — not slides, not a demo video, the actual system. Then we'll map it to your business and tell you honestly whether it's a fit. No pressure close. If it's not right for you, we'll tell you.
